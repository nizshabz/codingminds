who we are

Our mission is to change the way shipments are made. Let's face it, shipments can often be chaotic. Not anymore. We are here to change that. Whether you are moving your precious furniture or your expensive car, at Shipnext, we aim to be a one-point destination to meet all your shipping and moving requirements. We will help you get the best deal by giving you access to our directory of thousands of transporters across the nation.

How we work

Shipnext is committed to make moving goods simple. All you need to do is post your shipping requirements We then bring to your disposal the bids of some of the best transporters from a list of more than ten thousand transporters enlisted with us from all around the country. Choose the best deal and you are good to go. That’s why we are here; to bring you the best deals.

Order placements made easy

Once you have identified the perfect deal, you can accept it with an easy click. Our transporter will then come to the location specified by you and pick up the shipment. And it doesn't just stop at that. You can track your shipment in real time and monitor its status by using our state of the art tracking technology which is installed in the vehicles of all our transporters.
