FAQ

1. Can I select any transporter who bids on my shipment?
Yes. You can choose the right deal from the list of bids. Each bidder will put up their price, time taken to ship etc. You can also choose the best transporter on the basis of the ratings they have received.
2. Can I cancel a post?
Yes, you can cancel your post up to 6 hours prior to the pick up time. For any cancellations post that, kindly contact our customer service executive who will assist you.
3. What is your refund policy?

5. Will my pets be handled with care?
We at Shipnext, care for your furry friends. Once we pick up your pet along with their cage we will make sure they stay hydrated and comfortable through the journey. We will make sure that we connect you only to those transporters who are comfortable in handling pets, so that there are no problems. However, we recommend not to transport your pet if they are younger than 10 weeks or have any health issues. Also, please consult with your vet for any precautions prior to travel. It will be a good idea to get your pet micro-chipped and have an ID around their collar along with you name, address and phone number.
6. What is the best way to describe my shipment?
It is best to describe your delivery in the most accurate possible. For example, while moving house hold god and furniture, the best thing to do would be to measure height, width and length as an estimated cumulative. It is better for the weight, however to be close to accurate, since prices vary according to weight.
